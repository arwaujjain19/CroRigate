ABOUT US

CropRigate

CropRigate come with a number of important features that are necessary to facilitate smart irrigation. Individual software products come with different features depending on the vendors.

CropRigate allows farmers, homeowners and others to automate their irrigation activities and optimize water usage.

The IOT devcies is able to regulate water levels depending on various factors.

Such as weather predictions and soil moisture levels.

This is possible as the devices software communicates with our irrigation control system and identifies when the landscapes needs more water.

Automated and Customizable schedules make it possible to irrigate throughout the life-cycle of a crop from the planting to the harvesting seasons.

The system knows when to start or stop irrigating because of it's specially designed sensors.

The sensors play an important role in gathering useful data about the weather, temperature, soils moisture levels, humidity, and other factors.

The software uses this data to determine whether to irrigate or reschedule irrigation.

Additionally, the software collects data about other factors.

Such as soil condition plant water usage and wind conditons.

OUR SOLUTIONS

Smart Irrigation

Scheduled watering to meet specific needs of the landscape. Traditional irrigation operate on a programmed schedule and timers whereas smart irrigation system will monitor the weather, soil moisture and plant’s water usage sensor’s data to automatically adjust the watering quantity and schedule.

Smart Fertigation

Apart from irrigating the landscape, based on the soil conditions & crop being grown, appropriate nutrients needs to be delivered as well. Based on the inputs, proper concentrate of water soluble NPK can be concocted and delivered to the landscape, maintaining the soil nutritional value.

Smart pH Control

Along with the watering and fertigation, another important factor is soils' pH level. If appropriate pH isn’t maintained it will damage crops. Required alkaline or basic solution can be released to control Soils’ pH Level. So it protects crop from damaging and maintain soil fertility.
